About Andrew Gao

Andrew Gao is a Senior at Trinity Prep. Andrew completed the calculus cirriculum at Trinity in his junior year, and has qualified for AIME. Andrew is a 2x USNCO National Qualifier, 3x USABO Semifinalist and 2x Honorable Mention. He has competed as part of the Trinity Varsity History Bowl Team, which won 19th at nationals, and won 3rd in the National Biology Exam and 6th in the National Science Bee. In addition, he has published original research on leukemia and lymphoma. He is the developer of this website.
