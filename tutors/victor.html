About Victor Wang

Victor Wang is an incoming freshman at the University of Pennsylvania as part of the Vagelos Molecular Life Sciences Scholars Program. Victor scored a 1580 on the SAT and a perfect 36 on the ACT, and was a 3x AIME Qualifier as well as US Chem Olympiad National Finalist. He was captain of Science Olympiad, concertmaster of the Trinity orchestra, and president of Limitless Horizons of Florida, a non profit committed to teaching the underserved in rural China. Victor was part of the Mu Alpha Theta honor society, the Rho Kappa honor society, National Honor Society, Cum Laude Society, and was Valedictorian of his grade. He is available to tutor any science or math class, SATs or ACTs.
